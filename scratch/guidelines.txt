Academic Report Guidelines:
1. Chapters:
- Abstract: A concise summary of the academic project.
- Introduction: Background context and project relevance.
- Methodology: Algorithms, architecture, and design approach.
- Results: Evaluation metrics, statistics, and graphs.
- Conclusion: Summary and future scope.

2. Formatting:
- Citation format: IEEE numeric style.
- Typography: Times New Roman, 1.5 line spacing.
